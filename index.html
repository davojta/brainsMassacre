brains massacre

Description

In this game you need to fight with the gangsters by solving different logical and mathematical tasks.

There are two spells in the game: attack and healing. if you solve the task correctly, you apply the spell, otherwise the spell is applied by the gangster.

killing the gangster you move to the next round, in which the next gangster becomes stronger.

Let's see how many monsters you can win! good luck!
